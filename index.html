Purpose

Our company strives to sell stylish fashion at low prices. We also want to make the process of buying as simple as possible. No lock out due to loss of product, no waiting in line, just simple and easy fashion.

Audience

Our audience is to try and help those in need. We want to help lower class people look good without breaking the bank.

Q&A

Is the clothing high quality?

Our company strives to keep the clothing as cheap as possible while making them last long and stay clean and stylish. Our fabric may not be as good as other companies, but it's the best for the price presented.

What age range do you cover?

We mostly do clothing for teens and adults, but if contacted, we can arrange making something for kids younger, prices will depending on the age and amount of fabric used.

How long will this clothing last?

These sets can last around 2-3 years depending on how much it's worn. It mainly just depends on what you do in the clothing. The fabric itself is durable enough to withstand normal situations.

Is the clothing machine washable?

Yes, these clothing were made to be washed in the washer and dryer.

Why chose Fast Fashion over other cheap fashion companies?

Fast Fashion goes for the lowest possible price. Other cheap fashion brands will pay lots of money for terribly made clothing that will break after a month. We do the most to get the prices as low as possible while keeping the fashion fresh and durable.

Do I need to worry about products being sold out?

No, the site won't ever lock you out of something due to it being sold out. Even if we're out of the product, we'll ship the order as soon as we get the product. We also won't get rid of a product at anytime. So you don't have to worry about running out of time to purchase something.

Can I return the purchase if I'm not satisfied?

If you provide proof of purchase. And send pictures of what is wrong with the product then we will happily work with you to get your money back to you.
